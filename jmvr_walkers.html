Each walker is one realisation of the continuous-time KMC. With many walkers, the cloud profile approaches P(x, y, t). Try this: drop γ to 0.005 and pull ε to its max — directors persist, walkers shoot in straight lines along the lattice axes, giving the 4 or 6 ballistic rays. Crank γ to 0.3 with ε ≈ 0 — directors randomise fast, walkers spread isotropically (square Gaussian / round hexagonal).
